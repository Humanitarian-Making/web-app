Goods Received Note

One of the key components of manufacturing and distributing items locally is the Goods Received Note. This is used to confirm the delivery of items to those in need, as well as estimate the quantity of people that were affected by the item - useful to gauge your impact. We have a sample electronic Goods Received Note here. You can download this XLSform file and use it to share your work.
